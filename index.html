Our Legacy

The story of TranStar

  • 1963/1964

    Project Axiom

    A joint U.S.-Soviet initiative establishes the Kletka facility — the first dedicated site for non-terrestrial research.

  • 1980

    The Pobeg Incident

    Following an unforeseen containment irregularity, Project Axiom is suspended and the Kletka facility is decommissioned.

  • 2025

    TranStar Established

    The TranStar board of directors is founded. Kletka is acquired and earmarked for state-of-the-art refitting as a private research facility.

  • 2030

    Talos I

    The newly designated Talos I opens as TranStar's flagship research facility. The first commercial Neuromod enters the market.
